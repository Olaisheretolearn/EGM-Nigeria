    April 2024
    Theme : Competency Based Assessment (CBA) Hands-On Training
    Saturday, 27th April 2024
    Chairman: Johnson Aina P.Eng., M.Sc., PMP
    Moderator: Secretary & PRO
    Location: In-person @ACOMI EVENT HALL
    In this event, we covered the essentials of Competency Based Assessment (CBA) through interactive training. Highlights included understanding the competency framework, the importance of experience in CBA, and adapting to Canadian standards. The session concluded with a hands-on CBA report workshop, providing practical skills for participants. It was a valuable session tailored for newcomer engineers, offering insights for professional integration and growth
    Vote of Thanks from the Secretary

    As we reflect on our recent Competency-Based Assessment (CBA) Training workshop, I am filled with immense gratitude. Your participation and enthusiasm have not only contributed to the success of this event but have also reinforced the spirit of our professional community.
