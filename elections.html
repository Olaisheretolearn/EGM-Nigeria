    Council Election
    The council election is held every two years to elect the new executive that will run the affairs of the Nigerian Chapter EGM. The council election is backed by the Nigerian Chapter’s constitution and only eligible candidates can via for elective positions if they meet the eligibility criteria.
    Election Process

    The election process starts with constituting an election committee that oversee and conduct a free and fair election. You can vote in the election if you are a member of the Nigerian Chapter member with an eligible membership type at the time of the election and in good standing with EGM.

    What you need to know

    For you to vote or get voted for, you need to know the following so that you do not get disenfranchised.
